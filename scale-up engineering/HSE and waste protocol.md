# HSE and Waste Management Protocol

# 1. Process Waste Profile (Per 100 kg Batch)
* Process Water (Byproduct): 5.92 kg of water generated.
* Nature:Acidic due to trace p-Toluenesulfonic acid (PTSA) and organic volatiles.
* Treatment: Collected via Dean-Stark separator, neutralized with Sodium Carbonate (Na2CO3), and sent to ETP.


# 2. Equipment & Process Safety
* Material of Construction (MOC): SS316L (Stainless Steel) to resist catalyst corrosion at 108°C.
* Pressure Management: Spring-loaded Pressure Relief Valve (PRV) set at 2.0 bar.
* Ventilation: Local exhaust required during Salicylic Acid charging to manage dust.

# 3. Sustainability Metrics
* Atom Economy: ~64%.
* Resource Circularity: 90% Alcohol Recovery recovers 131 kg of Tridecyl Alcohol per batch for reuse.
* Impact:Reduces net raw material waste by >60%.

# 4. Required PPE
* Nitrile/Neoprene Gloves: For handling PTSA and finished ester.
* Thermal Gloves: Mandatory for sampling at the 108°C setpoint.
